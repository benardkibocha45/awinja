About Awinja Nyamwalo

Jacky Vike History

Awinja Nyamwalois Kenya's most beloved househelp with attitude — known for her witty comebacks, exaggerated rural charm, and iconic Luhya-English accent. She rose to fame on the hit TV show Papa Shirandula, where her hilarious take on everyday Nairobi life made her a household name.

Behind the Awinja character is the incredibly talented Jacky Vike, an award-winning actress, content creator, and humanitarian. Born and raised in Nairobi, Jacky has transformed Awinja from just a TV role into a cultural icon — using humor to highlight social issues, celebrate African culture, and connect with fans across generations.

From acting and comedy skits to red carpet appearances and brand campaigns, Awinja continues to break boundaries while keeping it 100% local, 100% funny. Whether she's on stage, screen, or your social feed — one thing's for sure: with Awinja, laughter is guaranteed.
